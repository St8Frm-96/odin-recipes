Homemade Pizza

The best homemade pizza to ever come out of your wimpy, not-wood-fired oven!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (12-16oz)
  • Olive oil (1 tablespoon)
  • Pizza sauce (1 cup)
  • Thinly sliced mozzarella cheese (3oz)
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese (5oz)
  • Pepperoni (2.5oz)
  • Shredded parmesan cheese (2 tablespoons)

Recipe instructions

  1. Place a pizza stone in the bottom of your oven.
  2. Preheat the oven to 550 degrees F.
  3. Prepare a work surface.
  4. Roll out the dough.
  5. Transfer the dough to a square of parchment paper.
  6. Par bake.
  7. Top your pizza.
  8. Bake the pizza.
